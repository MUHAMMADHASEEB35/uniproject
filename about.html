Our Story

Traditional Recipes Since 2025

Sialkoti Restaurant was founded in 2025 by Muhammad Haseeb in the heart of Sialkot. What began as a small eatery serving traditional Pakistani dishes has now grown into a beloved restaurant chain with branches in Islamabad, Wah Cantt, and Sialkot.

Our commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods has remained unchanged over the decades. Each dish is prepared with the same care and attention to detail that made our original location so popular.
